# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Solar Powered Storage System for Perishable Goods in Nigeria project and its community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and inclusive of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuroatypicality, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion
- Deliberate intimidation
- Stalking or following
- Harassing photography or recording
- Sustained disruption of community spaces
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. We are committed to maintaining the safety and respect of all community members.
